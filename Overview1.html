2019/20 Season Champions Crowned. 2 Records Set in the Process

Liverpool have been crowned as Premier League champions after a thrilling game between Chelsea and Manchester City which Chelsea won 2-1.

It has been 30 years since they last won the league.

Manager Jurgen Klopp deserves all the credit after taking a team with some terrible issues and to make them the best they have possible ever been.

After this game Manchester City was second and trailing by 23 points with only 7 games left.
